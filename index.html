Privacy Policy — Notie

Last updated: February 2026

Notie (“the App”) is designed to help users capture and clarify their thoughts while respecting their privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use Notie.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Overview

Notie is a privacy-first application. The App does not require user accounts, does not track users, and does not collect personal information for advertising or analytics purposes.

All data created within the App is intended to remain under the user’s control.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Notie does not collect or store:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials (no account creation required)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data for marketing or profiling purposes

The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

3. Voice Input and Speech Processing

When you choose to create voice notes, Notie uses Apple’s system-provided speech recognition and language processing APIs to convert spoken audio into text.

  • Audio processing is handled by Apple’s system services
  • Notie does not record, store, or transmit audio files to its own servers
  • Speech processing behavior is governed by Apple’s platform privacy practices

Notie does not independently access, retain, or share voice recordings.

4. Apple Intelligence

Some features of the App rely on Apple Intelligence to perform on-device content processing.

  • These features are only available on supported devices
  • Processing is performed using Apple’s system frameworks
  • Notie does not have access to the underlying models or system-level processing

Availability and behavior of Apple Intelligence features are subject to Apple’s platform requirements and settings.

5. Data Storage

All notes and content created in Notie are stored locally on the user’s device.

  • No cloud synchronization is performed
  • No external servers are used to store user data
  • Data remains on the device unless explicitly deleted by the user

Users may delete individual notes or clear all app data at any time through the App’s settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Notie does not share user data with third parties.

The App relies only on Apple-provided system services (such as speech recognition and on-device intelligence). Any data handled by these services is governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

7. User Control and Data Deletion

Users maintain full control over their data.

  • Notes can be edited or deleted at any time
  • All stored data can be permanently removed from the device via the App’s settings

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

8. Children’s Privacy

Notie does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Because the App does not require accounts or collect personal data, no child-specific data is stored.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable regulations.

Any updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top of this document.
