Notie is designed with privacy in mind. Your thoughts are personal, and they stay that way.

1. Data Collection

Notie does not collect, track, or sell personal data.

  • No accounts are required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No third-party advertising services are included

2. Voice & Speech Data

When you use voice notes, Notie uses Apple's system speech APIs to convert your voice into text.

  • Audio is processed by Apple's system services
  • Notie does not store or transmit your audio recordings
  • Speech processing behavior follows Apple's platform privacy policies

3. Data Storage

All notes are stored locally on your device.

  • No cloud sync
  • No external servers
  • You can delete all data at any time from within the app

4. Apple Intelligence

Some features rely on Apple Intelligence to process content privately on-device.

  • These features are only available on supported devices
  • Processing is handled by Apple's system frameworks

5. Third-Party Access

Notie does not share data with third parties.

Any system services used (such as speech recognition) are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

6. User Control

You are always in control of your data.

  • You can delete notes individually
  • You can clear all stored data from the app
